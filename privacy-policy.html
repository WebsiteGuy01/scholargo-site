Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2025

Let’s be real — nobody *wants* to read a privacy policy, but we’re glad you’re here because your data matters. Here’s how we at ScholarGo handle things behind the scenes.

🔍 What We Collect

We only collect the basics:

  • Your name or email (if you willingly give it to us — like subscribing or contacting us)
  • Some anonymous stuff like which pages you visit, via Google Analytics
  • We might show ads (hello, AdSense), which could track stuff using cookies

🎯 What We Use It For

We use this info to:

  • Make the website better
  • Reply if you send us messages
  • Sometimes show you relevant ads or scholarship suggestions

🙅 What We Don’t Do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't spam your inbox
  • We don’t stalk you — promise

🧁 Cookies (the digital kind)

Like most websites, we use cookies — not the chocolate chip kind — to improve your experience. You can turn them off in your browser settings if you want.

🔐 Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to know what info we have about you (if any), and you can ask us to delete it. Just email us and we’ll sort it out — no drama.

🧰 Third Parties

We might use services like:

  • Google Analytics — to understand how people use our site
  • Ad networks (e.g., AdSense) — to help keep the lights on

These may collect anonymous data through cookies or pixels.

📅 Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (because laws or tools change), we’ll update this page. So feel free to check in once in a while if you're curious.

📫 Contact Us

Questions? Concerns? Cookie recipes? Email us at admin@scholargo.netlify.app and we’ll get back to you.

Thanks for trusting ScholarGo 💙
